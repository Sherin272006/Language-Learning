Common English Statements

Everyday Expressions

  • Hello! - A common greeting.
  • How are you? - A way to ask about someone's well-being.
  • Thank you! - An expression of gratitude.
  • I'm sorry. - An apology.
  • Can you help me? - A request for assistance.
  • What time is it? - A question to ask for the current time.
  • See you later! - A casual way to say goodbye.
  • Have a great day! - A positive farewell wish.

Polite Requests

  • Could you please pass the salt?
  • Would you mind closing the window?
  • Can I have a glass of water, please?
  • May I use your phone?
  • Could you help me with this task?

Common Questions

  • What is your name?
  • Where are you from?
  • How old are you?
  • What do you do for a living?
  • What is your favorite food?
